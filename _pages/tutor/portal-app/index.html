Portal App Features

Your central hub for managing all aspects of FormLang. Administrators can control users, permissions, billing, and system settings. All users can access reports, forms, and personalize their experience.

Administration

  • User and Role Management
  • Organization Unit Permissions
  • Audit Logs
  • Billing and Payment
  • System Settings (Logo, Security, etc.)
  • Schedule Jobs (Emails, Text Messages)
  • Subscribe to Events and Configure Actions
  • Configure Google and Microsoft SSO
  • Dashboard Reports (Usage, Forms, etc.)
  • Visitor Group Reports
  • Browse Organization Forms, Flows, and Workflows
  • Form Response Reports (Single, Flows, Workflows)
  • Import/Export Data
  • Response Action Center (Approve/Reject, Send Messages)

Personal

  • View Personal Form Responses
  • View Recently Visited Links
  • View Favorite Links
  • Update Profile and Password
  • Configure Two-Factor Authentication
