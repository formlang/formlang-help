Get started with FormLang

FormLang is a powerful form design system that helps businesses, families, and individuals create customizable and shareable forms. It is portable, expressive, reusable, and community-supported.

FormLang is designed to streamline operations and enhance productivity for organizations. It provides a variety of features for both organization administrators and employees. Please refer to the guides fitting your needs.

Organization Administrators With FormLang, you can create and manage forms, automate workflows, and collect data from employees, visitors, and anonymous users. FormLang is easy to use and scalable, making it a great choice for organizations of all sizes. Click to learn about how to create your organization account. Employees Employees can use FormLang to find out the available forms, form flows, and/or workflows, submit and view their responses to these crafts. Click to learn about how to join your organization.

Quick start for organizations

Creating your organization account.


  1. Visit the FormLang Portal.
  2. Click the Register link at the bottom of the page.
  3. On the Create your organization account page, follow the instruction to enter the required information.
  4. An email will be sent to the email address you provided. This email will contain an activation link.
  5. Click on the activation link in the email.
  6. You will be taken to the FormLang Portal. Your organization account will be ready.
