Get started with {{ site.formLangBrand }}

{{ site.formLangBrand }} is a powerful form design system that helps businesses, families, and individuals create customizable and shareable forms. It is portable, expressive, reusable, and community-supported.

{{ site.formLangBrand }} is designed to streamline operations and enhance productivity for organizations. It provides a variety of features for both organization administrators and employees. Please refer to the guides fitting your needs.

Organization Administrators With {{ site.formLangBrand }}, you can create and manage forms, automate workflows, and collect data from employees, visitors, and anonymous users. FormLang is easy to use and scalable, making it a great choice for organizations of all sizes. Click to learn about how to create your organization account. Employees Employees can use {{ site.formLangBrand }} to find out the available forms, form flows, and/or workflows, submit and view their responses to these crafts. Click to learn about how to join your organization.

Quick start for organizations

Creating your organization account.

  1. Visit the {{ site.formLangBrand }} Portal.
  2. Click the Register link at the bottom of the page.
  3. On the Create your organization account page, follow the instruction to enter the required information.
  4. An email will be sent to the email address you provided. This email will contain an activation link.
  5. Click on the activation link in the email.
  6. You will be taken to the {{ site.formLangBrand }} Portal. Your organization account will be ready.

Quick start for employees

There are two ways to join your organization:

  • The administrator creates the account for you.
    In this case, you will receive the emails about your account.
  • you join your organization via the member registration link
    . To join your organization, please follow the general instructions below:
    1. First, the system will ask for your organization name. Please contact your administrator for the organization name. (The system will remember your organization information in your browser.)
    2. Next, the system will ask for your personal information, including your name, email, and password. Once you submit the personal information, you account will be created.
    3. Then, you may receive an email including the activation/confirmation link to your account. Please use the link to confirm your email and/or activate your account.
